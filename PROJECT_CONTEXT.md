# What This Project Is

The Financial Analytics Platform is a treasury operating platform designed for community banks.

It is **not** a dashboard.

It is **not** a reporting application.

It is **not** a BI project.

The platform exists to transform raw accounting information into trusted financial information through a validation-first, metadata-driven workflow.

Reports are consumers of the platform.

They are not the platform itself.

---

# Mission

Reduce the amount of time treasury professionals spend preparing information.

Increase the amount of time they spend making decisions.

---

# Core Philosophy

The platform follows one principle above all others.

> **If the data cannot be trusted, every downstream report is worthless.**

Everything else exists to support this principle.
